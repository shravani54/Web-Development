Mumbai

Mumbai is India's financial capital and the most populous city, known for its vibrant culture, bustling streets, and iconic landmarks like the Gateway of India. It is also home to Bollywood, the heart of the Indian film industry.

Pune

Pune is a cultural and educational hub in western India, known for its historic landmarks and thriving academic institutions. Often called the "Oxford of the East," it offers a blend of tradition and modernity.

Delhi

Delhi, India's capital, is a dynamic blend of history and modernity, with landmarks like the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and India Gate. It's a political, cultural, and commercial center, bustling with diverse communities and vibrant markets.

Bangalore

Bangalore, now Bengaluru, is India’s tech capital, known for its booming IT industry and start-up culture. The city is also renowned for its pleasant climate, lush green spaces, and vibrant café culture.

Noida

Noida, a planned city in Uttar Pradesh, is known for its rapid urbanization, modern infrastructure, and thriving business hubs. It is a major IT and industrial center, offering a blend of commercial growth and residential development.
